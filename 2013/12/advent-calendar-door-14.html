Advent Calendar Door 14

Dec 14th, 2013

Dear Santa,

with December weather not being able to make up its mind, I leave behind my trailrunners and am in need of some boots. Yes, some ankle high boots should be in this slush/ rain/ snow and cold mix weather just right, and ECCO makes just the shoe I had in mind: The ECCO BIOM Terrain. It’s available for Women and Men, and both have a Gore-Tex® lining to keep the slush away from the feet as well as a Yak leather upper that survives scrambles on sharp rocks. Oh, and great grip they have too. These should be great in the snow & slush, and my warm and happy toes will thank you for your kindness!

Sincerely yours,

An aspiring lightpacker
