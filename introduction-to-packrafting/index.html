Introduction to Packrafting

May 3rd, 2013

Packrafts open up new possibilities for your wilderness adventures. Where before you walked on trails, now you can float down rivers and cross lakes instead of needing to hike around them. A complete package of packraft, paddle, and PFD can add as little as four kilogram to your load, which if put into comparison with the options it opens up for your adventures if very little indeed. In this course you learn the fundamentals of packrafting, and at the end of the course you will be able to confidentially paddle White Water up to Class II, cross lakes with your raft and how to look in a new way at maps to plan your adventures.

Itinerary of this course:
Day 1:
- Meeting in the early morning and travelling together to the starting point of our trip
- Basic paddle instructions: How to inflate the packraft, how to get in and out of it with dry feet, paddle technique
- Learn to paddle on a lake or slow flowing river
- Preparation of camp, pitching shelters, dinner

Day 2:
- Full day of packrafting
- Learning to scout rapids & paddle them down
- We paddle down a river, and will camp out again in the evening

Day 3:
- We pack up and paddle out
- Post-trip meal, optional sauna
