Video is the medium of the internet, as it is so easy to upload and distribute it. Also most cameras nowadays have a video option, so even for the uneducated it is easy to make and edit a movie. My favourite Outdoor video channel is definitely that of shugemery aka Sean. I love his humour and impersonations, they give a very nice touch to his videos and make them very enjoying to look. While I am not an hammock and tarp backpacker, his videos always inspire me to give it a try. Browse his Youtube channel or watch the video underneath, as usual, click HQ to see it in a better quality.


Winter Warmth Accessories with Shug ..."Live From The Hammock"

The second video I would like to share today is the trailer of the "Tell it on the Mountain" documentation. It documents the trip of five Pacific Crest Trail Thru-Hikers, and has stunning pictures and insights of extraordinary people. The movie is at the moment in post-production, and hopefully is available as a DVD by the end of this year. Also have a look at their other videos. Make sure to click on "HD on" if you watch it. But be careful, you might want to walk the PCT after seeing this ^_^
