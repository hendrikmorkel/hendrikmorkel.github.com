I was planning to go this weekend for a trip to Isojärvi National Park, which is also close to Tampere, however, both my heels still have open blister so walking is rather painful. Which is a shame, really, as outside the sun is shining and Temperatures are -3°C and are expected to drop to -8°C tonight, which would have been an excellent opportunity to test my winter sleepingbag under good conditions - last weekend it was too hot.



Isojärvi is a nice national park for weekend trips, it has a few lean-to shelters which mean you don't need to bring a tent - which is good news for me, as I don't have one yet. Anyhow, up the map of the Isojärvi NP. I reckon my feet should be fine till next weekend, thus if I don't go to Helsinki I will go and have a good time in Isojärvi. Starting in Huhtala its a nice trip to walk till Vahterjärvi, stay there for the night and then continue the next day to Heretty. From there it should be easy to hitch a ride back home to Tampere.
