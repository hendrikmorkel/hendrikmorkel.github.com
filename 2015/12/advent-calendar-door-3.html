Dear Santa,

staying warm in winter - it’s something we Northern people know about. Hailing from Canada Arc’teryx belongs to the illustrious circle of companies which know how to keep alpinists, skiers and backpackers warm when the temperatures drop below 0°C. Their Atom LT Hoody is not just one of their most popular midlayer insulation pieces which is great when cold wind howls through the mountains & tundra, it also works well on its own when it is not freezing cold. Coreloft™ insulation in the torso, arms and hood keep one toasty, and the side panels of Polartec® Power Stretch® with Hardface® Technology add breathability and stretchiness. And when the sun comes out, it packs small and light in the pack - excellent for a winter day out in the hills!
