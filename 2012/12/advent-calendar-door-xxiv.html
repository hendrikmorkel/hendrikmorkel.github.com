Advent Calendar - Door XXIV

Dec 24th, 2012

Dear Santa,

it’s a bit short notice, today being Xmas et al. though I wanted to let you know that I have found the perfect backpack for me - the Aarn Mountain Magic 50. With large front-back volume ratios, a body-centered load is easy to achieve which means that balance and posture are unaltered by the load - perfect for hiking on narrow ridges, duckboards and any place where a good balance is needed. With the Balance Pockets you can have all what you might need during the day right in front of you, and they’re also 100% Waterproof. Being from New Zealand they might be a tad far away for you to fetch on such quick notice, though I found out that Backpackinglight.dk is selling them in Europe, which might mean a shorter trip for you!

Merry Xmas!
An aspiring UL Backpacker
