Advent Calendar - Door I

Dec 1st, 2012 | Comments

Dear Santa,

after a year of sleeping on the ground I decided it is time to lift myself of the floor and try a hammock. I read a smart book about hammocking and figured out that a good suspension system is what makes or breaks a good nights sleep, and thus I’d like to ask you for a pair of Whoopie Slings hammock suspension system in my choice of colour & size for my hammock. That should ensure an easy setup of my hammock and keep me hanging high!

Thank you, and a fine start into December!

An aspiring UL Backpacker
