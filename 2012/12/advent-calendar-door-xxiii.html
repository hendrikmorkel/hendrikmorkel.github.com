Advent Calendar - Door XXIII

Dec 23rd, 2012

Dear Santa,

now that you brought me a wonderfully warm down hat, my wife is loaning it all the time and I still get cold ears! I realized the only way to get my down hat back is to get her a own one. The Aria Beanie from Outdoor Research would be perfect for her needs, I think - light, warm, and stylish it is what a woman wants, if you ask me! It would be great if you could send me one for her!

Happy Fourth Advent,
An aspiring UL Backpacker

As the world didn’t end, you might need a warm head for the winter ahead; and I got two to give away! Check your size, fill in the form and keep your thumbs up!
