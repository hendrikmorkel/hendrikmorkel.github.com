Winter Backpacking Immersion

Nov 22nd, 2012

You would like to reinforce your lightweight winter backpacking skills and go on the trip of a lifetime? Then the Winter Backpacking Immersion week (seven days/ six nights) is the right trip for you. We will head far up north, and experience the Arctic wilderness, off the beaten track. Witness the Northern Lights, sleep in a snow shelter and leave nothing behind but the tracks of our skis this is an experience you will remember for years to come.

From the 16.03. till the 23.03.2013 we will head to the beautiful Urho Kekkonen National Park and ski for a week through it’s breathtaking landscape. This land, close to the Russian Border, is home to Wolverines, Lynx, Wolves and Bears, and we might see tracks from them in the snow. However, more likely we are to see Reindeer, Elks and Hares, while in the sky above us we might be able to spot a Golden Eagle. This will be a superb trip to strenghten your winter skills, and experience the beautiful Nature of Lapland!

Price: 1.050,00 € per person
Date: 16.03. - 23.03.2013
Location: Urho Kekkonen National Park, Lapland

The last day to enrol for the Winter Backpacking Immersion course is the 17th of February 2013.
