Ultralight Backpacking Basics

May 3rd, 2013

© bmp

The Ultralight Backpacking Basics course is aimed at backpackers who would like to experience the benefits of backpacking in a lightweight manner. Besides the possibility to loan and try different ultralight gear, you also learn to navigate off-trail, and cooking with different fuels.

Summer is undoubtedly the best time to be backpacking outdoors: Warm weather, flowering plants, shady trees and singing birds make for an enjoyable time in the forests and hills. With a lightweight backpack this will be even more fun, as instead of looking down at your shoes under a towering load, with a light backpack you will be able to view the surroundings and have a smile on your face!

Among other things, in this course you learn how to

  • Select appropriate gear for a summer trip
  • Stay warm, dry and comfortable hiking and at camp
  • Pitching your shelter

Itinerary of this course:
Day 1:
- Meeting in the early morning and travel together to the starting point of our hike
- Hike for two to four hours before we start to look for a good place to camp
- Preparation of camp, pitching shelters

Day 2:
- Full day of hiking
- Navigating in the summer forest
- Building a fire

Day 3:
- We pack up and hike out
- Post-trip meal, optional sauna

After this course you will possess the skills and knowledge to plan your own summer trips and will feel confident to hike & camp in ultralight style.

Dates in 2015:
In 2015 there is one set date for groups of maximal six participants. These are a great way to meet fellow lightweight backpacking enthusiasts from across the globe and learn UL skills.
