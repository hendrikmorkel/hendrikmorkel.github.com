Introduction

Apr 13th, 2009

Hi there. I decided to start a separate blog for my Outdoor adventures, as it is a slightly different topic of what I write and think about over on my first blog. Thus here just all about hiking in Finland, although also hiking in Norway and Sweden will be covered in due time! I’ll give you tips and tricks concerning what to think about if you plan a hiking trip to Finland, where to go, what to take and also will be available to answer your questions. I’ll also talk about gear, because that’s what a hiker talks about if he’s not hiking =)

To kick things off, I will start this week with a report from my trip to the Helvetinjärvi National Park last weekend, and will translate my report from the Karhunkierros last fall. I’m planning already my next trips, and I am quite lucky as Tampere is very close to several very beautiful National Parks.
