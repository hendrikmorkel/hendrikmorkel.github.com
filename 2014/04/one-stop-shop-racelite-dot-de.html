One Stop Shop: Racelite.de

Apr 8th, 2014

Racelite.de is a shop specializing in trailrunning and lightweight backpacking gear. As trailrunners go fast & light, you can be sure that their list is going to be interesting!

Located at the foot of the Alps in Garmisch-Partenkirchen Racelite.de has its website and Brick & Mortar shop open since 2005. Robert Pohlhammer is certainly a well-known name in Germany and beyond, as he is the organizer of the Yukon Arctic Ultra and the Trail Days. This is reflected also in part in his list:

My thoughts on the list:

  • Great sleeping system combo
  • A bit heavy choice for the backpack, but it’s pretty high volume
  • Nice shelter choice, though I’d have liked to see a tarp again
  • Now that stove & pot combo is interesting, versatile as it can use Esbit, wood or alcohol as fuel and packs very compact
  • Interesting shoe choice, never heard of the brand before but seems like a good shoe
  • Excellent choices for garments!
  • Again a very well-rounded list that’s pretty affordable - the cheapest European list thus far!
