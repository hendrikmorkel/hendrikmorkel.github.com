Advent Calendar - Door 20

Dec 20th, 2011



Dear Santa,

climbing is fun, let me tell you. I boulder on my own a few times each week, and toprope with a friend a couple of times per month. Now I am looking forward to take it to the next level, and start lead climbing. That’s when you ascend a route from down up and clip in your rope while going up. One needs so-called Quickdraws or Express sets for this, and it just so happens that Mammut makes the very light Bionic Evo Express Sets. I imagine four of them should have me covered for the start =)

Best,


- An aspiring UL-Backpacker
