Prices, Registration & Discounts

Mar 30th, 2013

Prices

Introduction to Packrafting 500€
Ultralight Backpacking Basics 400€
Ultralight Backpacking Immersion 1.000€

Included:
- Around the clock guiding & instructions
- Breakfasts and dinners
- Gear discounts from partners
- Possibility to loan gear, e.g. stoves, shelters & tarps, quilts & mats, backpacks
- Packrafts, paddles, and PFDs for the packrafting courses

Not included:
- Daytime food & snacks
- Transportation
- Personal gear
- Insurance

Registration

Once you have picked a course you’d like to join you enroll via the Registration Form. Registration for a course closes three weeks before it takes place or as soon as the course is full. It’s in your best interest to enroll as soon as possible to guarantee your spot and help me plan the courses.

After you filled in the registration form you’re paying a non-refundable deposit. These are via Bank Transfer. PayPal, as long as no extra fees are incurred to me, is also possible. When I receive your registration form, you will get an Email with payment details and more information.

Non-refundable deposit:

Introduction to Packrafting 200€
Ultralight Backpacking Basics 100€
Ultralight Backpacking Immersion 300€

The outstanding amount is due 21 days prior to the start of your course.

Gear Discounts

If you enroll for a course you qualify for gear discounts from these webshops and cottage gear manufacturers:

  • 15% off from Hyperlite Mountain Gear products
  • 10% off from your Trekking-Lite-Store.com order

You’ll receive your discount codes in an Email after your non-refundable deposit payment has cleared.

No Gear? No Problem!

If you enroll for a course but lack a piece of equipment, you have the possibility to loan it - for free! - from me for the duration of the course. After all, sometimes you’d like to try something to see if it is the right piece of gear for you. Loans are on a first come, first serve basis, so if you’d like to loan something you should possibly already let me know when you enroll!
