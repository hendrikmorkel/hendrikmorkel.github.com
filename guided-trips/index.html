Guided Trips

Oct 11th, 2012

In summer and autumn 2015 there are various opportunities to learn about ultralight backpacking and packrafting in Northern Europe.

Introduction to Packrafting course | 2016
If you want to learn to packraft, there’s no better place than Finland. We start by learning about packrafts and paddling on the lakes before we take our skills to the rapids (up to Class II). Packrafts, paddles, and PFDs are all included in the course, and we’ll be using lightweight gear to camp. Read more.

Ultralight Backpacking Basics course | August 2015
The Ultralight Backpacking Basics course takes place in the south Finland, which is easy to reach thanks to direct airplane-connections to Tampere and Helsinki. You’ll learn about ultralight gear, pitching different shelters, cooking with alcohol, Esbit and wood and 3-Season Sleeping Systems, amongst other things.

Ultralight Backpacking Immersion course | 2016
If you want to see the beautiful Tundra of Northern Finland, the Ultralight Backpacking Immersion course will be to your liking. Not only will we hike through a gorgeous landscape of rugged fjells, there also plenty of lakes and rivers at which we will stop and enjoy some tenkara fly fishing. You’ll see that on this week long wilderness hike off the beaten track trailrunners, light backpacks and tarps are working very well while we navigate from North to South through the wilderness. Group size is six persons max, Price & Location TBA.
